Consultancy Services

Ecotrunk International Ltd offers a wide range of consultancy services aimed at supporting organizations in achieving their goals efficiently and sustainably. Our expertise spans various domains, ensuring tailored solutions for every client.

Project Feasibility Studies

We conduct comprehensive feasibility studies to assess the viability of projects, providing clients with clear insights into potential opportunities, risks, and strategies for success.

Surveys & Technical Assessments

Our team performs detailed surveys and technical assessments to ensure projects meet required standards, are efficient, and align with stakeholder expectations.

Environmental Management

We provide guidance on managing environmental and social impacts, ensuring compliance with regulations and fostering positive community relations throughout the project lifecycle.

Monitoring & Evaluation

Our consultancy includes robust monitoring and evaluation services to track project progress, measure outcomes, and recommend improvements for enhanced performance.

Training & Facilitation

We offer training and facilitation services to build capacity and equip teams with the skills necessary to achieve project objectives efficiently and effectively.

Resource Management

Our consultancy extends to managing natural resources responsibly, ensuring sustainable practices that balance economic growth and environmental preservation.
