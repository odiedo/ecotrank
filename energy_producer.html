Energy Sales

As an Independent Power Producer, in 2023 Ecotrunk produced and sold 4.3 TWh of renewable energy worldwide, an increase of 18% compared to 2022. This is equivalent to the electricity consumption of 5.5 million people. Our production is monetized through feed-in tariffs, tender-defined prices, wholesale market sales, and final customer sales via Corporate Power Purchase Agreements (CPPA).

Power Purchase Agreements (PPA)

In 2023, we secured a total capacity of 4.1 gigawatts, demonstrating the geographical diversification announced in 2019. This includes 46% in Europe, 43% in Latin America, and 11% in Africa. Companies consuming green electricity through Corporate PPAs represented more than 40% of our client base in the first half of 2024.
