Project Development & Management

Site Identification & Feasibility Studies

We carefully select the best locations for power generation by conducting resource assessments (using advanced software and campaigns) and evaluating production capacity. During this phase, we also assess potential environmental and social impacts, identifying solutions for land and grid connections.

Environmental & Social Studies

Ecotrunk rigorously evaluates all project sites to understand the potential impact on the environment and local communities. Our studies also include mitigation measures to minimize impact while fostering sustainable local development.

Grid Connection Studies

Grid connection is essential for a successful renewable energy project. Ecotrunk performs detailed assessments of the power infrastructure at substations where plants will be connected, ensuring seamless integration.

Project Design

Our engineering team designs plants to optimize power production based on site constraints, available resources, and grid capacity, ensuring efficiency throughout all development phases.

Land Securing

Land plays a critical role in renewable energy projects. We collaborate with landowners, public or private, and engage with local communities to ensure public acceptance. Negotiations include agreements and rents for land use.

Permitting

Compliance with local regulations is paramount. Ecotrunk secures licenses, permits, and other authorizations from regulatory bodies early in the project lifecycle to avoid delays and maintain smooth progress.
