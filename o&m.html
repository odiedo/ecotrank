Operation & Maintenance

Ecotrunk is a trusted international provider of Operation & Maintenance services. From diagnostics to ongoing operational management, we ensure seamless performance. Our 24/7 real-time monitoring and control systems enable rapid response across solar, wind, biomass, hydro, and storage plants in over 20 countries.
We also provide complete asset management services, ensuring sustainable, reliable returns on your investments while supporting a greener future.

What Sets Us Apart

Employees

People profiles focus on long-term electrical maintenance expertise. Voltalia employees are at the core of our operations.

Processes

Systems

Advanced systems like Solar Hub (Portugal), Wind Hub (Brazil), and VoltPower System ensure seamless operations. Customers access their data through the Energy Portal (EP).
