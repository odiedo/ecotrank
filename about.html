Ecotrunk International Ltd (ETL)

ETL is a firm of Technologists, Consultants, and Managers incorporated in the Republic of Kenya as a firm of Experts. The firm has broad experience in Project Development & Management, Engineering Systems Development and Consultancy in the renewable energy industry.

Our Mission

To provide the most competitive energy, engineering, and construction consulting services and tools in the respective industries incorporating an advanced approach

Our Values

Integrity

Integrity is at our core. We deliver sustainable value through dedicated teams, respect for stakeholders, and sound guidance for informed decisions. Honesty and commitment shape our corporate culture, reflecting high ethical standards across our actions.

Ingenuity

Innovation drives renewable energy projects. Since 2014, we’ve combined expertise and creativity to succeed, including securing major wind projects in different countries. Ingenuity remains key to our culture and success.

Entrepreneurship

We support renewable energy projects at every stage, using agility and proactive approaches to meet client goals. Founded in 2014, we maintain an entrepreneurial spirit, embracing opportunities with a flexible organization.

Team Spirit

Our diverse global teams engineers, environmental experts, and more—collaborate for the success of each project. From planning to operations, teamwork has been the backbone of our energy transition efforts.
